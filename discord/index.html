Discord Presence

Live Discord status on your Xeneon Edge — avatar, activity, custom status, and Spotify now-playing. No bot, no OAuth, no secrets.

Setup — one-time
  1. In Discord: Settings → Advanced → Developer Mode ON.
  2. Right-click your avatar → Copy User ID.
  3. Join the Lanyard server: discord.gg/lanyard (required — stay in the server).
  4. Paste your User ID above and hit Connect.
  5. For Spotify now-playing, make sure Discord shows you as listening (green equalizer on your profile).
