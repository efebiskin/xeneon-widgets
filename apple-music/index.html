Apple Music

Paste any Apple Music share link — song, album, playlist, station, or radio. Plays 30s previews for everyone, full tracks for subscribers signed into Apple's embed.

Or pick a station
How to get the URL
  1. Open Apple Music (web, iOS, or Mac).
  2. Find a song/album/playlist → ⋯ menu → Share → Copy Link.
  3. Paste it above. Accepted formats: music.apple.com/*/album/*, /playlist/*, /song/*, /station/*.
About HomePod control: Apple does not expose HomePod playback over any web-callable API — the only official path is the Home/Shortcuts apps on an Apple device. The practical workaround is Home Assistant with the HomeKit Controller integration running on a Mac/Raspberry Pi on your network; it exposes a local REST endpoint a Xeneon widget can hit. Until that's set up, this widget controls an Apple Music embed — not the physical speaker.
